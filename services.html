Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive mobile auto glass solutions, including windshield chip and crack repair, full windshield replacement, side/rear window replacement, and ADAS camera recalibration.

How long does a service appointment take?

Most chip repairs take about 30 minutes. Full replacements usually take 60 minutes of labor, plus a recommended 60-minute "Safe Drive-Away Time" for the adhesive to cure properly.

Do I need to provide anything for the service?

Our mobile units are fully self-sufficient. All we need is about 3 feet of clearance around your vehicle and, if it's raining, a garage or carport to keep the work area dry.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book instantly through our website's booking form, or call our 24/7 hotline at 1-800-GLASS-FIX. We'll often have a technician available the same day.

Can I reschedule or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel at no cost up to 2 hours before your appointment window. Simply use your dashboard link or call us directly.
