Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept all major insurance providers. In fact, we are a preferred vendor for most insurers, meaning we can often repair your windshield with $0 out-of-pocket cost to you.

What documents do I need to submit a claim?

All we need is your insurance provider name and policy number. Our team will handle the rest, including contacting your agent and filing the glass claim paperwork.

How long does the insurance claim process take?

Verification is usually instant during business hours. We can often confirm coverage and schedule your appointment within minutes of receiving your policy details.

Is my vehicle/property covered during the service?

Absolutely. We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. Your vehicle is covered by our professional liability insurance from the moment we start working until we finish.

Who do I contact for insurance-related queries?

You can call our dedicated insurance support desk at 1-800-GLASS-IMS or use the live chat on your customer dashboard for real-time claim status updates.
